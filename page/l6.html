King Of Gluttony

Summary

King of Gluttony follows Sebastian Laurent, the charming heir to a culinary empire who appears confident and successful but hides deeper insecurities beneath his polished exterior. Throughout his life, his greatest rival — and secret obsession — has been Maya Singh, a smart, competitive marketing executive who challenges him at every turn. When unexpected circumstances force them to work together, the tension between them intensifies, blurring the line between hate and undeniable attraction. As they spend more time in close proximity, both Sebastian and Maya must confront their feelings and grapple with how much they truly mean to each other. Ultimately, the story explores how rivalry can transform into passion, showing that sometimes your greatest enemy could become your greatest weakness — and your greatest love.
