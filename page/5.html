Once Upon A Broken Heart

Summary

Once Upon a Broken Heart tells the story of Evangeline Fox, a young woman whose heart is shattered when the boy she loves is about to marry her stepsister. Desperate to fix her broken heart, she makes a dangerous bargain with Jacks, the Prince of Hearts, agreeing to his mysterious conditions. This deal pulls her into a magical and treacherous world full of curses, fate, and unexpected challenges. Along the way, Evangeline must navigate deception, test her courage, and question what she truly wants in love and life, discovering that happily ever after may come at a high price.
