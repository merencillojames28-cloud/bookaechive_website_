King Of Envy

Summary

King of Envy follows Vuk Markovic, a powerful and reclusive billionaire who has always avoided deep relationships — until he finds himself drawn to Ayana Kidane, a glamorous supermodel engaged to his oldest friend. Though Ayana’s engagement is actually a business arrangement to secure her freedom from an abusive agency, Vuk struggles with loyalty and desire as his feelings for her grow. As their connection deepens, both must face the tension between passion and propriety, even though being together could upend their lives. When Ayana’s wedding plans collapse and danger looms, Vuk becomes both her protector and temptation. In the end, the story explores forbidden attraction, emotional vulnerability, and the cost of pursuing love against all odds.
