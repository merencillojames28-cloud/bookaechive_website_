The Sweetest Oblivion

Summary

The Sweetest Oblivion follows Elena Abelli, a young woman from a powerful crime family who hides her troubled past behind a sweet exterior. Her life becomes complicated when she is drawn to Nicolas “Nico” Russo, a dangerous and charismatic mafia boss. Despite family expectations and loyalty, a forbidden attraction ignites between them, pulling them into a world of secrets and tension. As they navigate betrayal, power struggles, and high-stakes danger, Elena and Nico must confront their feelings and decide what they are willing to risk for love. In the end, the story explores how passion and peril can intertwine, and how love can emerge even in the darkest circumstances.
